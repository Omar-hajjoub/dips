The Challenge

Why Water Treatment Struggles

Traditional water treatment facilities face mounting pressure from energy costs, quality requirements, and operational complexity.

High Energy Costs

Desalination and water treatment consume massive energy. Pumps, membranes, and thermal processes drive OPEX through the roof.

40-60% of total OPEX is energy

Inconsistent Water Quality

Fluctuating feedwater quality, membrane fouling, and scaling lead to unpredictable output quality and compliance risks.

±15% typical quality variance

Reactive Operations

Operators struggle with manual adjustments. By the time issues are detected, damage is done and costs have escalated.

3-5hrs average response delay

Result: Unoptimized plants waste up to $2M+ annually in excess energy, chemicals, and maintenance.
